MY CHILDHOOD

from 2002 to now.

2002

I was born in the autumn of 2002 and came to this world with the anticipation of my parents. But later on, my father told me that I was so ugly when I was born, hahaha, all the children who were just born looked like this.

2003

In 2003, I was one year old. I vaguely remember the SARS outbreak back then, when someone sprayed disinfectant on our doorstep. That year, our whole family also moved into a new house.

2004

When I was two years old, I took a set of very festive photos and looked really happy at the time.

2005

When I was three years old, I started attending kindergarten. I looked forward to my future life before the start of school, but I cried a lot at the beginning of school. Now it's really an interesting experience to think about.

2006

The first time I had an impression of traveling was to Mount Taishan. Many people went together, and I couldn't climb halfway until I finally reached the top of the mountain by cable car.

2007

The adults were too busy to take care of me, and the kindergarten happened to have a painting class. I spend my weekends drawing, and the feeling of casually splashing paint has made me addicted to drawing.

2008

The Olympics were held in this year, and at that time, I was too young to have any impressions of the opening ceremony of the Olympics.

2009

When I was seven years old, my cousin got married. I went to serve as the flower boy for her wedding, and on that day, I was wearing a white dress. At that time, I felt like I was a beauty.

2010

I started to come into contact with musical instruments, starting with electronic organs. At the age of eight, I also began to learn various interests, but I gave up learning Latin dance. But I learned a lot of other things!

2011

I don't have many memories of this year, I feel like the memories of nine years old are always blurry.

2012

I am ten years old! That year, we also went to the seaside to play, but there was a slight accident during the process. My mother had an allergy to seafood, and we urgently went to the hospital. At that time, I was scared. However, the entire journey was quite enjoyable.

2013

At the age of 11, I am in fifth grade and once again in the final exam, I won my first first place in life. I still remember when my father was very happy and invited me as a guest. To be honest, the feeling of being first place is really great!

2014

In sixth grade, we moved to the new campus. The sixth year of primary school ended when I was twelve years old, and my paintings even won awards.

2015

My junior high school life started from this year!

2016

During my summer vacation, traveling to South Korea was the first time I felt like I could do many things beyond my imagination. In addition, I have also accomplished many things that I want to do this year.

2017

In 2017, I faced graduation once again. At that time, I spent almost half a year preparing for the exam and was under a lot of pressure. At this time, I didn't know how to handle the pressure.

2018

In the year of entering high school, I also experienced many setbacks. It was also in this year that our whole family officially decided to support me in learning design. Although this matter had been planned since junior high school, this was the official decision.In this year, our family once again moved to a new home.

2019

My mother and I went to Yunnan for an unforgettable tour. The natural landscape is unforgettable.

2020

In 2019, I started studying professional knowledge in Beijing. But in the upcoming New Year, the epidemic broke out, and for the first time, I went home alone by plane. In the following year, the epidemic trapped me and I couldn't go to school, so I had to take online classes at home.

2021

Between 2020 and 2021, I underwent several exams that were crucial to me. After the college entrance examination, I went to the northwest to play for a long time. I received the admission letter during this period, and my previous efforts were not in vain!

2022

When I first entered university, I felt very confused and always made mistakes in many unexpected places. However, university is also beautiful, and these beauties always appear in unexpected corners.

2023

Recently, I have always been experiencing some anxious emotions. But after careful consideration, I have decided! I am going to see a richer world and have decided to study abroad. Send yourself beautiful blessings. My life has been filled with love from birth to now, and I also thank my parents.
